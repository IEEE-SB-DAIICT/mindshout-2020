About MindShout

i.Fest is the largest technical fest of Gujarat.

Over 20 events are organised where established personalities are invited from every industry to interact and provide endeavouring insights to young minds for over a decade now.

With a vision of reaching out to school students and introducing them to the latest applications of mathematics and science in real life, i.Fest is proud to present MindShout 1.0 as the first step in this direction.

MindShout 1.0 is an MCQ-based Aptitude Test for school students aimed at students of Classes 8 to 12.

Why MindShout?

Why MindShout?

Aptitude Testing

MindShout is an excellent opportunity for students to test themselves in a competitive environment and get them prepared for national competitive exams like JEE, NEET, CLAT etc.

National Level Test

We take this opportunity to reach out to students all across India and allow them to test themselves at a national level and get a nationwide ranking.

Questions designed by Experts

With questions designed and curated by experts, this test aims to challenge the thinking engines of students and provide students with a platform to prove their mental powers.

Two Stages

  • Verbal Reasoning

  • Single Choice Questions

  • 4/5 options - 1 correct

  • Difficulty based on the current grade/standard of the student
